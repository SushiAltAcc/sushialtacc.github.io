Reloaded7 system requirements

If you want to run Reloaded7 on your PC, here's what it takes (Recommended):

  • 2.5 gigahertz (GHz) or faster 64-bit processor

  • 8 GiB (8192MiB) of RAM

  • 40-60 GB available disk space

  • DirectX 9 or later graphics device with WDDM 1.0 or higher driver

Minimum specifications:

  • 1 gigahertz (GHz) or faster 64-bit processor

  • 4 GiB (4096MiB) of RAM

  • 20 GB available disk space

  • DirectX 9 graphics device with WDDM 1.0 driver

Additional requirements to use certain features:

  • Internet access (fees may apply)

  • Depending on resolution, video playback may require additional memory and advanced graphics hardware

  • Some games and programs might require a graphics card compatible with DirectX 11-12 for optimal performance

  • For some Windows Media Center functionality a TV tuner and additional hardware may be required

  • Windows Touch and Tablet PCs require specific hardware

  • HomeGroup requires a network and PCs running Reloaded7

  • DVD/CD authoring requires a compatible optical drive

  • BitLocker requires Trusted Platform Module (TPM) 1.2

  • BitLocker To Go requires a USB flash drive

  • Windows XP Mode requires an additional 1 GB of RAM, an additional 15 GB of available hard disk space, and a processor capable of hardware virtualization with Intel VT or AMD-V turned on

  • Music and sound require audio output

Product functionality and graphics may vary based on your system configuration. Some features may require advanced or additional hardware.
